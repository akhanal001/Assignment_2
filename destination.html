Travelogue: Outer Banks

Overview

The Outer Banks is a string of barrier islands off the coast of North Carolina, offering a mix of natural beauty and historical significance. Visitors can enjoy activities such as beachcombing, fishing, water sports, and exploring historic sites like the Wright Brothers National Memorial and Roanoke Island.
